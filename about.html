Our Mission

EcoSort was created to eliminate "wish-cycling" and help communities clean the planet one item at a time.

How it works

1. Snap a photo of your trash and the bin.

2. Our AI verifies if it's the correct disposal method.

3. Watch your virtual forest grow as you build healthy habits!

Did you know? Putting the wrong item in a recycling bin can contaminate an entire truckload of clean recycling.
